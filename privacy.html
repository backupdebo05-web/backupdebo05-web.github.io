Personal Identifiable Information (PII)

Personally Identifiable Information (PII) refers to any information that can be used to identify an individual. This may include, but is not limited to, a person’s name, email address, phone number, or other contact details.

Information We Collect

Peace-Thrives Homes LLC collects limited personal information through our website contact form. This information is provided voluntarily by users and is used solely for communication and inquiry response purposes.

Use of Information

Information submitted through our contact page is used to respond to questions, provide requested information, and communicate regarding our services. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Data Retention

Emails and contact information received through our website are retained only as long as necessary for communication purposes. If an individual does not become a client or utilize services through any Peace-Thrives Homes LLC property, their information will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable timeframe unless retention is required for legitimate business or legal purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Cookies and Website Usage Data

This website uses limited technical cookies that are necessary for basic functionality and security. These cookies do not collect personal identifiable information and are not used for tracking, advertising, or analytics purposes.

By using this website, visitors consent to the use of essential cookies required for normal operation. Users may disable cookies through their browser settings; however, some website features may not function properly.
