Privacy Policy

Personal Identifiable Information (PII)

Personally Identifiable Information (PII) refers to any information that can be used to identify an individual. This may include, but is not limited to, a person’s name, email address, phone number, or other contact details.

Information We Collect

Peace-Thrives Homes LLC collects limited personal information through our website contact form. This information is provided voluntarily by users and is used solely for communication and inquiry response purposes.

Use of Information

Information submitted through our contact page is used to respond to questions, provide requested information, and communicate regarding our services. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Data Retention

Emails and contact information received through our website are retained only as long as necessary for communication purposes. If an individual does not become a client or utilize services through any Peace-Thrives Homes LLC property, their information will be deleted in a reasonable timeframe.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.
