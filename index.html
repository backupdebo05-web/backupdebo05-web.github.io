Welcome

Peace Thrives LLC provides private or shared residential housing accommodations designed to promote stability, dignity, and a safe living environment.

Our focus is on housing access and residential accommodation and helping adults receive the non-medical services they need. We do not provide medical, nursing, or health care assistance. Individuals seeking accommodation with us must be capable of independent living and managing their own daily activities, and make individualized necessary medical arrangments if required.

Monthly rates include

  • Furnishing
  • Wi-Fi
  • Water
  • Light
  • Gas
