About Peace Thrives

Peace Thrives LLC operates as a residential housing provider and also offers non-medical support services such as light housekeeping, errands, and related non-medical assistance. We do not provide healthcare services, personal medical assistance, or medical supervision.

Individuals seeking residential accommodation with us must be capable of independent living and managing their own daily activities. Residents who require medical or personal care services must make their own arrangements with qualified providers. Non-medical support services may be offered under a separate agreement.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create safe, stable, and respectful residential living environments where adults can thrive with dignity, independence, and a strong sense of community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality private and shared residential housing accommodations while supporting independent living through structured, non-medical support services that promote stability, responsibility, and personal growth.

Who We Serve

Peace Thrives serves adults who are capable of independent living and are seeking private or shared residential housing accommodations. Our housing options are designed for individuals who value a supportive living environment but do not require medical, nursing, or personal care services.
