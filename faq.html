Frequently Asked Questions

What type of housing does Peace Thrives provide?

Peace Thrives offers private and shared residential housing accommodations. Our properties are designed to provide a stable and comfortable living environment for individuals seeking housing options.

Do you provide medical, nursing, or personal care services?

NO. Peace Thrives provides housing accommodations only. We do not offer medical care, nursing services, personal care assistance, medication management, or health-related supervision.

Who is eligible to apply for housing?

Applicants must be capable of independent living and managing their own daily activities. Housing availability and eligibility are determined through an intake screening process based on housing guidelines and availability.

What is the intake screening process?

The intake screening process is used to determine housing availability, suitability, and compliance with housing guidelines. Submission of an inquiry or requesting for residential accommodation does not guarantee acceptance or approval for housing.

Are private rooms available?

Private room availability varies by property and occupancy. Availability is subject to change and is determined during the intake screening process.

Are shared living accommodations available?

Shared room accommodations may be available depending on property capacity and current occupancy.

Is housing furnished?

Furnishing details vary by property. Specific information regarding furnished or unfurnished accommodations can be discussed during the inquiry process.

Are utilities included?

Utility arrangements vary by property. Details regarding utilities will be provided during the housing inquiry or screening process.

How long can someone stay in the housing?

Length of stay depends on housing availability, occupancy agreements, and compliance with housing guidelines. Specific terms are discussed prior to move-in.

Is there a waitlist?

Housing availability may be limited. When applicable, interested individuals may be placed on a waitlist and contacted if housing becomes available.

Do you offer transportation, meals, or other services?

NO. Peace Thrives does not provide transportation, meals, healthcare, or personal assistance services. Residents are responsible for arranging these independently.

How do I inquire about housing availability?

Housing inquiries can be submitted through our website contact form. All inquiries are reviewed as part of our intake screening process. Individuals or agencies may choose to email us directly or call our phone line.

Do you follow fair housing laws?

Yes. Peace Thrives complies with applicable fair housing laws and does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, disability, familial status, national origin, or any other protected characteristic under applicable law.

Peace-Thrives provides housing accommodations only and does not offer medical, nursing, or personal care services.
