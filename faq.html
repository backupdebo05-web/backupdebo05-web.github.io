Frequently Asked Questions

What type of housing does Peace Thrives provide?

Peace Thrives offers private and shared residential housing accommodation. Our properties are designed to provide a stable and comfortable living environment for individuals seeking housing options.

Do you provide medical, nursing, or medication care services?

NO. Peace Thrives offers housing accommodation and non-medical related suppport services. We do not offer medical care, nursing services, health care assistance, medication management, or health-related supervision.

Who is eligible to apply for housing?

Adults who are capable of independent living and managing their own daily activities. Housing availability and eligibility are determined through an intake screening process based on housing guidelines and availability.

What is the intake screening process?

The intake screening process is used to determine housing availability, suitability, and compliance with housing guidelines. Submission of an inquiry or requesting for residential accommodation does not guarantee acceptance or approval for housing.

Are private rooms available?

Private room availability varies by property and occupancy. Availability is subject to change and is determined during the intake screening process.

Are shared living accommodations available?

Shared living accommodation may be available depending on property capacity and current occupancy.

Is housing furnished?

Furnishing details vary by property. Specific information regarding furnished or unfurnished housing can be discussed during the inquiry and intake process.

Are utilities included?

Utility arrangements vary by property. Details regarding utilities will be provided during the housing intake process.

How long can someone stay in the housing?

Length of stay is a minimum of 6 months but depends on occupancy agreements, and compliance with housing guidelines. Specific terms are finalized prior to move-in. We do not offer transient housing or accommodation.

Is there a waitlist?

Housing availability may be limited. When applicable, interested individuals may be placed on a waitlist and contacted if housing becomes available.

What non-medical services does Peace Thrives offer?

Peace Thrives offers non-medical support services such as light housekeeping, errands which include grocery shopping assistance, Pharmacy pick-ups (no medication handling or administration), and related services.

How do I inquire about housing availability and non-medical services?

All inquiries can be submitted through our website contact form. Received inquiries are reviewed and responses provided within 24hrs. Individuals or agencies may choose to email us directly or call our phone lines.

Do you follow fair housing laws?

Yes. Peace Thrives complies with applicable fair housing laws and does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, disability, familial status, national origin, or any other protected characteristic under applicable law.

Peace Thrives LLC offers housing accommodation and provides non-medical support services. We do not provide medical, or nursing care services.
