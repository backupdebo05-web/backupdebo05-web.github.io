Our Services

Peace Thrives LLC offers residential housing accommodations for individuals seeking private or shared living arrangements, along with non-medical support services that help promote a stable, safe, and dignified living environment.

Residential Housing Accommodations

  • Private room accommodations (subject to availability)
  • Shared room accommodations (subject to availability)
  • Safe and maintained residential environments
  • Clear housing guidelines and occupancy expectations
  • Coordination of move-in and housing availability inquiries

Non-Medical Support Services

  • Light housekeeping (routine cleaning of living areas and common spaces)
  • Errands and community support (including grocery shopping assistance)
  • Pharmacy pick-ups (no medication handling or administration)

Services We Do Not Provide

Peace Thrives LLC is a non-medical housing accommodation provider. We do not provide medical, nursing, home health, or personal care services. Residents must be capable of independent living and managing their own daily activities.

  • No medical care, nursing care, home health services, or clinical assessments
  • No medication administration, medication setup, dosage management, or injections
  • No wound care, catheter care, or ostomy care
  • No monitoring of vital signs (blood pressure, glucose checks, etc.)
  • No hands-on assistance with bathing, toileting, dressing, feeding, or transfers/lifting
  • No physical, occupational, or speech therapy services
  • No emergency medical response services (call 911 for emergencies)

If a resident requires medical or personal care services, the resident (or their representative) is responsible for arranging appropriate licensed providers.
